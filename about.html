What we are

Founded in 2017, in Lisbon, Tesselo’s mission is to tackle environmental challenges using the power of satellite imagery and artificial intelligence.

Forecasting and mitigating vast phenomena such as climate change starts with measuring them at scale and quantifying their effects. We design solutions to support businesses in managing their land resources in a safer, more efficient and sustainable way.

What we do

Our daily job consists in turning environmental data into actionable spatial intelligence.

Our environmental engineers, agronomists and industry specific experts translate natural patterns into spatial models.

Our AI engineers decompose the models into layers of information from a combination of sources such as satellite imagery, aerial imagery, drones, multi-Spectral, SAR and LiDAR.

These layers of information are then aggregated and analyzed through time. Our system engineers automate layer production and develop custom features tailored to your business’ needs: alerts, intelligence reports, integration into existing solutions.

Raw

Tesselo absorbs a variety of raw data from various sensors such as satellites, drones, radars and multispectral imagery.

Clean

We produce crystal clear composite imagery: Tesselo composes mosaics from dozens, sometimes hundreds of photos, removing clouds and shadows, calibrating each pixel to atmospheric conditions.

Intelligent

We run AI models to generate satellite imagery that pinpoints your business’ specific problem, and we then monitor incremental changes through time, helping you identify patterns, exposure levels and risk factors.
